The Dark Knight Rises is a 2012 British-American superhero film directed be Christopher Nolan, who co-wrote the screenplay with his brother Jonathan Nolan, and the story with David S. Goeyer. Featuring the DC Comics character Batman, the film triology, and the sequel to Batman Begins (2005) and The Dark Knight (2008). Christian Bale reprises the lead role of Bruce Wayne/Batman, with a returning cast of allies: Michael Caine as Alfred Pennyworth, Gary Oldman as James Gordon, and Morgan Freeman as Lucius Fox. The film introduces Selina Kyle (Anne Hathaway), a sly, morally ambiguous cat burglar, and Bane (Tom Hardy), a revolutionary bent on destroying Gotham City who forces an older Bruce Wayne to come out of retirement and become Batman again.
